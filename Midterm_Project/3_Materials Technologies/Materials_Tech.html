    The program is aimed to develop the shortest path from fundamentals in mechanics and physics to industrial applications with a particular focus on materials and novel technologies. It prepares the next generation of researchers, engineers, and practitioners to assume the role of technical leadership in research and innovation. Students learn the scientific fundamentals and state-of-the-art technologies in the areas of advanced composite materials, additive manufacturing, and thermal spray coatings. Our graduates design and test materials, and apply them in large-scale industries.

    Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology


    Skoltech is a new global research university created in collaboration with MIT. The new campus, designed by Herzog de Meuron, is home to 40 world-class labs with best-in-class equipment, globally renowned professors and students from 40+ countries.

    Education

    The MSc program is 2 years long: the first year is to strengthen your theoretical background, and the second year is to focus on research. Students have the freedom to choose courses and extracurricular activities to shape their individual trajectory, acquire soft skills, and gain entrepreneurial skills to prepare for employment.
    Lectures and practical classes conducted by world-renowned professors and experts
    Students' individual research projects carried out at Skoltech laboratories.

    An 8-week summer industry immersion program at leading companies turning knowledge and skills into action.

    Courses on entrepreneurship and innovation that provide skills, as well as knowledge, to commercialize ideas and research findings.
    + Academic mobility at leading global universities (on a competitive basis)

    ADMISSIONS

    Our program provides students with extensive theoretical and practical knowledge in materials technologies. Our graduates will have:

    fundamental knowledge in mechanics and physics of composite materials, additive manufacturing and thermal spray coatings

    important practical experience of working with cutting-edge equipment

    deep understanding of modern technological methods and tools, information-technology-based manufacturing and product development practices

    ability to apply their knowledge of mathematical methods, mechanics, physics, and material science in engineering practice and research in advanced manufacturing and materials
