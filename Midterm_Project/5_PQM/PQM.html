    Photonics is among the most important areas of modern science and technology. Our MSc program provides world class education and research in photonics, bio-photonics and nanoelectronics – our students will be prepared to apply fundamental science and engineering approaches to higher-level research and industrial tasks. The program allows to customize your educational experience by selection of tracks: research-oriented, engineering-oriented, and industry-oriented (joinly with IPG Photonics).

    Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology


    Skoltech is a new global research university created in collaboration with MIT. The new campus, designed by Herzog de Meuron, is home to 40 world-class labs with best-in-class equipment, globally renowned professors and students from 40+ countries.

    his is a core scientific track of the program that combines in depth education in modern solid state physics and photonics with courses on innovation and commercialization. The track starts with three intensive prerequired courses on quantum mechanics, photonics and innovation that introduce student to the field and followed by a wide variety of elective courses allowing student to elaborate a track that fits his scientific interests in a best way. Access to the world-class laboratories and friendly and inspiring environment ensures that most of our graduates has at least one publication in high-impact journal.

    The track can be mastered jointly with MIPT. Application to both MIPT and Skoltech are required.
    +
    The track aims to educate students to work on the industrial oriented problems of modern photonics. In addition to the fundamental knowledge in photonics including classical and quantum approaches, the students are supposed to go through intense optical practicum and selected electrical, mechanical and software engineering disciplines. The thesis work on this track aims to solve industrial oriented problems and will introduce student to the state of art photonic technologies and allow to acquire experience in communication with typical industrial processes.

    The track can be mastered jointly with MIPT. Application to both MIPT and Skoltech are required.
    +
    IPG Photonics is the world leading developer and manufacturer of high-performance fiber lasers and amplifiers for diverse applications in numerous markets. Students of these track will be engaged in projects related to the development, production and application of high power laser systems and new laser technologies based on fundamental scientific and applied engineering principles. The research part of the track is devoted to the development, production and introduction of high-tech products of the IPG Photonics to the international markets. Students of this track intern in the company and receive additional scholarship from IPG Photonics.

    Education

    The MSc program is 2 years long: the first year is to strengthen your theoretical background, and the second year is to focus on research. Students have the freedom to choose courses and extracurricular activities to shape their individual trajectory, acquire soft skills, and gain entrepreneurial skills to prepare for employment.
    Lectures and practical classes conducted by world-renowned professors and experts
    Students' individual research projects carried out at Skoltech laboratories.

    An 8-week summer industry immersion program at leading companies turning knowledge and skills into action.

    Courses on entrepreneurship and innovation that provide skills, as well as knowledge, to commercialize ideas and research findings.
    + Academic mobility at leading global universities (on a competitive basis)
