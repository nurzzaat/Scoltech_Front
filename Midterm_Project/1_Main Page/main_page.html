    Skoltech is an international graduate research-focused university that was founded by the group of world-renowned scientists in 2011. Skoltech's curriculum focuses on technology and innovation, offering Master's programs in 11 technological disciplines. Students receive rigorous theoretical and practical training, design their own research projects, participate in internships and gain entrepreneurial skills in English. The faculty is comprosed of current researchers with international accreditation and achievements.

    2 years (full-time). Certified diploma

    Education

    Courses

    Students complete a minimum of required courses and then build their own curriculum competing by choosing from 200+ electives in various disciplines.
    Research

    Students conduct real research with full access to Skoltech's advanced research laboratories. This reseach can also make up their final master's thesis.
    Industry immersion and internships

    In the summer between 1st and 2nd years of study, students gain practical experience in an 8-week-long internship at leading corporate and research organisations.
    Entrepreneurship

    Each program includes courses about commercialising projects, intellectual property law and business skills to prepare Skoltech graduates in their ventures and careers.
    Academic mobility

    30-40% of all Skoltech students get the opportunity to study abroad and complete research in the laboratories of the world's top universities.
    Full immersion in an international environment: continuous communication in english during class, laboratory work and student events. The Skoltech student body is a multinational community from more than 40 countries. Strong student background: Skoltech's competitive admissions process guarantees that students will study in an intelligent and motivated environment.

    Research / Labs

    Every program dedicates time for students' independent research as a core part of their curriculum. Skoltech hosts more than 25 world-class laboratories. Students have full access to the facilities throughout their studies and can conduct research based on their interests.

    Campus / Atmosphere

    The Skoltech campus was created by a world-renowned architecture agency Herzog & de Meuron. Currently, it is one of the most unique university buildings in the world. The building is a new way to look at space where people study, do research, witness discoveries and change the world.

    The campus hosts students from more than 40 countries. The diverse community of cultures and mentalities guarantees a vibrant and active lifestyle. Skoltech's atmosphere allows students to find like-minded people for social initiatives and clubs.
